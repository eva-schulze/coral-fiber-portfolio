## How to Score

| Question Type | How it works |
|---|---|
| Yes / No | Yes = full points, No = 0 |
| Scale 1–5 | 1 = not at all, 5 = fully in place |

> **Note on weighting:** Each section is worth 25 points (plus 15 for Cultural Fit), but equal point distribution does not reflect equal urgency. Finance issues — especially those tied to funder deadlines — take absolute priority regardless of score. A low Finance score always triggers immediate action, even if the total score looks acceptable.

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Status | What it means |
|---|---|---|
| 0 – 29 | 🔴 **Critical** | Urgent intervention needed. Multiple areas are broken or absent. Start immediately. |
| 30 – 57 | 🟠 **Struggling** | Significant gaps affecting daily operations. Prioritise the lowest-scoring areas first. |
| 58 – 86 | 🟡 **Functional** | Core systems exist but are inefficient or incomplete. Focus on closing key gaps. |
| 87 – 115 | 🟢 **Solid** | Strong operational foundation. Focus on optimisation and scaling what works. |

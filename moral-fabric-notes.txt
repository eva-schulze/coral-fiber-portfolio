Moral Fabric helps nonprofits focus on their mission by handling essential back-office operations including Finance, Legal, HR, and Technology.

They function as an embedded operations team — not consultants — integrating directly with your tools (Asana, Google Workspace, Slack, etc.) and taking on defined roles with clear accountabilities.

SERVICES
--------

Finance & Reporting
  - Bookkeeping, cash flow forecasting, project budgeting
  - Organizational forecasting and annual reports

Legal & Compliance
  - Entity setup, ANBI registration, insurance management
  - Contract templates and GDPR compliance

People & Culture (HR)
  - Payroll, onboarding, HR policies, 360 feedback
  - Recruitment, ATS setup, learning & development
  - Holacracy implementation

Tech & Tools
  - Tool stack setup, nonprofit software discounts
  - Hardware procurement, AI literacy training
  - Security testing and website building

Strategy & Execution
  - OKR implementation, project management, meeting rhythms
  - Theory of Change writing, reporting dashboards
  - Hierarchy of Purpose

Additional Services
  - Fundraising & CRM: grant writing, impact reporting, CRM setup
  - Office Management: supplies, facilities, internet
  - Event Support: venue logistics, registration, budgeting
  - Volunteer Coordination: onboarding, shift planning, communications

APPROACH
--------
Moral Fabric provides proven operational patterns tested across organizations,
not invented from scratch for each client. Nonprofits stay in control and can
reassign any role internally at any time. They also weave AI into operations
to multiply team capacity and impact.

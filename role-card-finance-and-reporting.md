# Role Card — Finance and Reporting
**Coral Fiber — Holacracy Role Definition**

---

## Role Name
Finance and Reporting

## Purpose
Keeping client organizations financially healthy, accurately administered, and well-reported — so leadership can make confident decisions and funders can trust the numbers.

## Domain
- All bookkeeping systems and financial records for client organizations
- Finance policies and patterns
- Budget frameworks, forecasts, and cash flow management
- Funder and annual reporting

## Accountabilities
- Administrating finance for client organizations and creating the policies and patterns that govern financial processes
- Ensuring good forecasting and liquidity management, and maintaining precise and up-to-date bookkeeping
- Making annual reports for client organizations
- Budgeting projects and tracking actuals against projections
- Reporting to funders in line with their requirements and deadlines

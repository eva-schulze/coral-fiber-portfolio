# Role Card — Legal and Compliance
**Moral Fabric — Holacracy Role Definition**

---

## Role Name
Legal and Compliance

## Purpose
Ensuring client organizations are legally sound, properly registered, and protected — and that other roles can act confidently within a clear compliance framework.

## Domain
- Entity setup and legal registrations for client organizations
- ANBI registration and maintenance
- Insurance policies and coverage decisions
- Contract templates and contract management
- GDPR compliance documentation and data processing agreements

## Accountabilities
- Setting up legal entities for new client organizations where required
- Registrating and maintaining ANBI status for applicable clients
- Managing insurance needs across client organizations, ensuring appropriate coverage is in place
- Setting up and maintaining contract templates for client use (staff, vendors, partners)
- Ensuring GDPR compliance across client organizations, including data processing agreements
- Supporting other roles with knowledge and guidance on legal and compliance matters relevant to their accountabilities

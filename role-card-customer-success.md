# Role Card — Customer Success
**Moral Fabric — Holacracy Role Definition**

---

## Role Name
Customer Success

## Purpose
Ensuring every nonprofit client achieves meaningful operational progress and feels genuinely supported throughout their journey with Moral Fabric.

## Domain
- Client relationship management and all direct client communications
- Client onboarding process and supporting materials
- Client health assessments and satisfaction data
- Decisions about escalating, pausing, or closing a client engagement

## Accountabilities
- Managing the onboarding of new clients from signed contract through to first operational handover
- Conducting regular check-ins with active clients to monitor progress and surface blockers early
- Maintaining up-to-date client briefs, health scores, and open question logs for each engagement
- Coordinating internally with Ops Specialists to ensure service delivery matches client expectations
- Identifying clients at risk of churn and initiating recovery conversations before issues escalate
- Collecting and documenting client feedback to inform pattern development and product improvement
- Ensuring all clients have signed the required legal documents (service agreement, GDPR) before work begins

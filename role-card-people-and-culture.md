# Role Card — People and Culture
**Coral Fiber — Holacracy Role Definition**

---

## Role Name
People and Culture

## Purpose
Ensuring client organizations have the people structures, policies, and rhythms in place for their teams to work well together and grow.

## Domain
- All people-related policies and patterns across client organizations
- Onboarding and offboarding workflows and documentation
- Payroll processes and personnel administration
- Feedback and performance cycle design and execution

## Accountabilities
- Creating and updating policies and patterns around people topics, including onboarding, offboarding, and 360 feedback
- Picking up operational people questions and tasks for clients, such as long-term sick leave, pregnancy leave, and reintegration processes
- Running quarterly feedback cycles across all client organizations
- Processing payroll accurately and on time each month
